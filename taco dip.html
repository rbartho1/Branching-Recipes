Renee's Taco Dip

by Ashlyn Haas

Ingredients

  • 1 package french onion soup mix

  • 16 oz sour cream

  • 16 oz cottage cheese

  • 12 oz shredded lettuce

  • 16 oz bottle taco sauce

  • 2.25 oz canned olives

  • 2 cups shredded cheese (of choice)

  • extra toppings are optional: onions, peppers, tomatoes, etc.

Directions

Step 1

Combine sour cream, cottage cheese, and french onion soup mix in a bowl and spread evenly into a dish.

Step 2

Evenly spread taco sauce over the mixture and layer the lettuce, cheese, and olives.

Step 3

Add additional toppings to your liking.

Step 4

Chill the dip for at least 30 minutes until ready to serve.

Tip 1

Lasts up to 3 days in refrigerator. If you would like to extend the freshness, do not add toppings until each time it is used.
