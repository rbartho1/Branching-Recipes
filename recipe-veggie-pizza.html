Veggie Deluxe Pizza

by Michael Lugo
Original recipe by Dana Sandonato from Killingthyme.net

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough, homemade or premade
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Red onion
  • Green bell pepper
  • Mushrooms
  • Black olives
  • Grape tomatoes

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven according to the instructions for your pizza dough. If using homemade pizza dough, preheat to 450ยบ F.

Step 2

Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the pizza, leaving about an inch of space around the edges for a crust.

Step 3

Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella evenly over the sauce.

Step 4

Top the cheese with all of your veggies: red onion, green bell pepper, mushrooms, black olives, and grape tomatoes.

Step 5

Place the pizza in the oven and bake for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
